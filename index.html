Personal Infomation

Hello! My name is Sarah Khan (s3897667), im from Melbourne in Victoria Australia. I was born in the United States and had my early childhood spent in Tokyo Japan. Im Pakistani and Muslim and speak fluent Urdu, (I did learn a bit of Japanese, but have forgotten over the years), I spend most of my time playing video games like Genshin Impact and experimenting around with software’s such as Adobe After Effects., and I also try to consume quite a lot of media (films, anime, manga).

Interest In IT

Information Technology itself is such a broad field filled with great innovative systems. My interest in IT started way back in high school, I had always had a interest in video and photo editing. I would spend hours on different projects making and compiling random things, now I grown to working with adobe after effects and sometimes work with 3d objects, shape layers and more.

I chose to study at RMIT mainly from personal feedback I had gotten from my career advisors in high school, through networking and attending career counselling sessions where students from RMIT came with information on their courses. RMIT is also convince-wise a great place to be! I use to attend the ATARNOTES lectures that were held in building 80 and had found the campus and people around me just a great place to be in.

From my time here at RMIT I hope to be able to gain knowledge of this fast-growing industry of future thinking in the hopes of going out into the world and gaining a stable job for my future. I want to know more about fields such as cloud computing, gaming and cyber security as these peaks my interests.

Ideal Job

Visit the AWS JOB!!

AWS (Amazon Web Services) has a position open for a cloud engineer, giving a great opportunity to join their support team and ‘run their mission critical applications’ this position allows the person to further develop your career into innovative technology allowing the user to be a part of companies who use AWS. Through further research Amazon provides mentoring programs with experienced engineers to help further develop skills. This position is eye-catching as it would allow me to engage with customers as well as solve technical issues. As well as by applying solutions that would fit with customer needs.

This job has both basic and preferred qualifications such as having strong knowledge on Windows OS, scripting with languages such as Python, an understanding of networking protocols: (HTTP, TCP), oral and communication skills and client service skills. The preferred qualifications include a prior working experience with AWS in term of CloudFront and Glacier as well as an understanding of content delivery networks.

Currently I have a great understanding of streaming video (h264, mp4, webm), messaging and queueing experience, security concepts, standard methodologies, Windows OS and Administration skills.

To help obtain these skills and qualifications I will need to complete certifications such as The AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner examination which would help me gain a better understanding in AWS cloud architectural principles, but over time the specific field I want to work in might change which will need me to gain experience through doing many jobs alike.
